Thanks for choosing Mass URL Opener

Follow these simple steps to get started with your new extension

1

Pin the extension for quick access to the Mass URL Opener.

Click on the extensions icon (puzzle piece) in your browser toolbar, then click the pin icon next to Mass URL Opener.

2

Simply click on the extension icon to open the Mass URL Opener.

Once pinned, you can access Mass URL Opener anytime by clicking on its icon in the toolbar.

You're all set! Click on the Mass URL Opener icon whenever you need to open multiple URLs at once.
