Thanks for choosing Mass URL Opener!

Get started in just a few steps with your new extension

1

Pin the extension to your browser toolbar for quick access to the Mass URL Opener

Click the Extensions icon (the puzzle piece) in your browser toolbar, then click the pin icon next to Mass URL Opener to pin it for quick access.

2

Click the extension icon to launch the Mass URL Opener

Once pinned, you can access Mass URL Opener at any time by clicking its icon in the toolbar.

You're all set! Just click the Mass URL Opener icon whenever you want to open multiple links at once.
